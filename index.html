Where grit meets opportunity

At Level Ground (LG), urban youth cultivate grit and resilience through Mixed Martial Arts. They learn to apply these values 'off the mat' in the pursuit of their ambitions through our Student Trainer Certification and College Access programs.

Watch the Video

Why

Our Mission is to empower troubled teens, foster growth, and inspire change. Through our program, we provide support for personal development, resilience, and a brighter future. Investing in their potential, we create thriving communities. Together, we make a lasting impact on the lives of these young individuals.

Visit the Gym

Discover our dedicated MMA training facility. With experienced coaches, a safe and disciplined environment, and a focus on character development, we offer a unique opportunity for physical fitness, self-defense skills, and personal growth.

Have us visit you

Experience the convenience of MMA training at your doorstep. Our skilled instructors bring the gym to you, offering personalized sessions tailored to your goals. Train in the comfort of your home, save time, and achieve your fitness ambitions with our mobile MMA services.
